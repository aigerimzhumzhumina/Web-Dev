Recipe #1: Chicken Katsu

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4 portions
Ingredients Quantity
Boneless, skinless chicken breasts 2
Panko breadcrumbs 1 cup
All-purpose flour 1/2 cup
Eggs, beaten 2
Salt and pepper To taste

Step 1: Butterfly the chicken breasts and pound them to an even thickness. Season with salt and pepper.

Step 2: Coat the chicken in flour, dip into beaten eggs, then cover in panko breadcrumbs.

Step 3: Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat and fry the chicken until golden brown (about 3–4 minutes per side).

Step 4: Remove and drain on paper towels.

Step 5: Slice and serve with tonkatsu sauce and steamed rice.

Recipe #2: Classic Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 8 pancakes
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 1 cup
Sugar 2 tbsp
Baking powder 2 tsp
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1
Butter, melted 2 tbsp
Salt 1/4 tsp

Step 1: In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.

Step 2: In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter together.

Step 3: Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until just combined (do not overmix).

Step 4: Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake.

Step 5: Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook for another minute until golden brown. Serve warm with syrup and butter.

Recipe #3: Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 24 cookies
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 2 1/4 cups
Butter, softened 1 cup
Brown sugar 3/4 cup
Granulated sugar 3/4 cup
Eggs 2
Vanilla extract 1 tsp
Baking soda 1 tsp
Salt 1/2 tsp
Semi-sweet chocolate chips 2 cups

Step 1: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.

Step 2: In a bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

Step 3: In another bowl, beat softened butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until creamy.

Step 4: Add eggs one at a time, followed by vanilla extract. Gradually add the dry ingredients and mix until well combined.

Step 5: Stir in the chocolate chips.

Step 6: Drop spoonfuls of dough onto the baking sheet and bake for 9–11 minutes or until golden brown. Let cool before serving.
